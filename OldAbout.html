António Leitão
Mathematician, reluctant rust programmer and a washed-up volleyball champion.
I'm a PhD student at the Scuola Normale Superiore supervised by Nina Otter and Fosca Giannoti on various projects topological data analysis, climate science, explainable AI and animal communication. I am a collaborator at CETI and was previously part of the NPL research lab where I worked with Giovanni Petri.
My main interests are on Topological Data Analysis and Machine Learning.
